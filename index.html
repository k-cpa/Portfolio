A short story of the unknown

Before I speak, I must now let you know my creed, I cherish poetry and music’s gentle seed. All that soothes the soul and lifts the heart to start, I’ll wield it freely, with no qualms, as I profess my art.

One morning, I seek the reason of mankind's quest, What causes all these changes, which never seem to rest? In his unpredictable ways, can we not find a clue, To make him change, and see the world anew?

We then uncover the forces that lie at the core, Of racism, sexism, and the struggles we deplore. We realize the subtlety of actions to take, To make the world understand, without force, what’s at stake.

While crafting these thrilling, passionate tales, We stumble upon a vast reality that prevails. An imagined world that once seemed far away, Now swiftly becomes more central every day.

Hundreds, thousands of colored lines unfold, That seem more logical than the world we've been told. For here, no law prevails, but the rules that guide inside. Eager to be challenged, refined, and amplified.
